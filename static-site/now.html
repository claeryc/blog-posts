Now

A running log of what I'm up to — newest first. Inspired by nownownow.com.

April 2026

Italy — small towns, jazz, and a metal first

Just back from a trip to Italy with a friend. We skipped Rome in favour of smaller towns, which was the right call — less crowd, more atmosphere. Did our first proper camping trip tied into some hiking in the mountains.

Found a jazz bar that had the same energy as the one I stumbled into in Thailand. Also took my friend to her first metal venue, which felt like a fair trade.

Early 2026

Back at the day job

The gap year wrapped up and I'm back at my previous job. Settling back into a routine after a year of moving around is its own adjustment.

2025

Gap year — studying Mandarin in Nanjing

Spent the year living and studying Mandarin in Nanjing. Took classes, ate a lot of 麻辣烫, and documented it all here.

September 2025

Traveling China — seven cities

A longer stint moving around China: Qingdao, Rizhao, Chengdu, Xi'an, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, and Foshan. Coast, mountains, ancient walls, and Cantonese food — all in one trip.

April 2024

Thailand — the trip that started it all

Solo trip to Thailand — my second time there, but the first where I actually tried talking to people. Met a group of Chinese travellers at a jazz bar and ended up spending the week with them. Left with new friends and a plan to go to China.
